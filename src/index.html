Data Fluencies Theatre Project

(Machine) Learning to Be

If you want to dance, sing, or scream out your frustrations in response to being misrepresented, ignored, judged, discriminated against, or erased by society, institutions, or automated/technological systems, what does that performance look like?

Please whisper or type your response. Feel free to express it in any way that makes sense to you.

Your response is anonymous. No personal data is collected besides the written or audio response you submit.
