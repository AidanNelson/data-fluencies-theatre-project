(Machine) Learning to Be

“How can we co-exist equitably with AI?” Developed as part of the Data Fluencies Theatre Project and co-created by a team of artists, (Machine) Learning to Be is a hybrid, devised multimedia performance that seeks to challenge established systems of control and explore alternative modes of engaging with our data-filled world in ways that open possibilities for more equitable futures alongside AI.

Online and in-person, (Machine) Learning to Be takes audiences on a multi-world journey, featuring a cast of human and digital characters: Shadow, Secret Hyena, Scarlet/CamGirl/DeadName, and Binary Oracle. These diverse characters delve deep into the myriad facets, desires, concerns, and harms surrounding AI.

This online portal offers an interactive experience structured as a virtual scavenger hunt. You will explore virtual rooms filled with clues and elements that delve into the characters’ worlds and provide glimpses into the performance’s themes and creation process.
